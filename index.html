THE SILENT STATIC

One man. One vision. No compromise.

The Silent Static is the project of James Simmons. A 30-year IT veteran and lifetime amateur musician from Sandy Hook, Kentucky. I build guitar-driven hard rock the only way I know how. This is done with my own hands, my own code, and my refusal to pretend there’s a "real" band behind the curtain. I am exploiting multiple AI technologies to program this band into existence.

There is no illusion here. Just one stubborn programmer turning sleepless nights, personal demons, and the ghosts of rock legends past into chest-hitting anthems. These are the raw confessions of doubt, anger, inherited flaws, and hard-won clarities that come from them.

It is visceral. It is loud. It is human?
